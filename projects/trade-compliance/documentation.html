Academic Research & References

"EU-India Trade Relations: Opportunities and Challenges in the Post-Pandemic Era"
European Parliament Research Service, 2023
This comprehensive study analyzes the evolving trade relationship between the European Union and India, focusing on market access, regulatory alignment, and future cooperation prospects. Key findings include significant complementarities in manufacturing and services sectors.
"Customs Compliance and Trade Facilitation: Comparative Analysis of Italian and Indian Systems"
International Trade Journal, Vol. 45, Issue 2, 2023
Comparative analysis of customs procedures, documentation requirements, and trade facilitation measures between Italy (EU system) and India (developing economy system). Highlights efficiency differences and potential for mutual learning.
"Digital Transformation in International Trade: Case Studies from Italian and Indian SMEs"
Journal of International Business Studies, 2024
Examines how small and medium enterprises in Italy and India are adapting to digital trade platforms, e-certification, and automated customs procedures. Identifies key success factors and implementation challenges.
"Supply Chain Resilience: Italy-India Trade Corridor Analysis"
World Trade Organization Working Paper, 2023
Investigates supply chain vulnerabilities and resilience strategies in Italy-India trade flows. Proposes framework for risk mitigation and contingency planning in bilateral trade operations.
